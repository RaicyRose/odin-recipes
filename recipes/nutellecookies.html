NUTELLA COOKIES



An easy recipe that can be done easily and quickly, and that tastes delicious! Perfect for any occassion, or for a little treat.
  • Time|| 15min
  • Serves|| 1

INGREDIENTS

  • 120g of flour
  • 280g nutella
  • 1 whole egg

STEPS

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C
  2. Mix the flour, nutella and a whole egg together, and separate into equal sized, about half a cm heigh, cookie shapes.
  3. Once the oven has finished preheating put the cookies on parchment paper and into the oven.
  4. The cookies may take between 6 to 8 minutes to be done, so check regularly to prevent them from getting burnt. Take out the oven, let cool, and enjoy!
