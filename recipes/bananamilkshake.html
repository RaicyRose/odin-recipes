BANANA MILKSHAKE



A sweet fruity treat, especially refreshing during the hot summer days.



INGREDIENTS

  • 1 Banana
  • 1 Vanilla Sugar Sachet [ add extra sachet after every fourth person ]
  • 1/4L Vanilla Ice Cream
  • 1/4L Milk of choice

    • You can adjust proportions to taste.

STEPS

  1. Cut the banana in slices.
  2. Add all ingredients to blender.
  3. Blend till equal consistency throughout the beverage
  4. Pour into glasses and enjoy!
