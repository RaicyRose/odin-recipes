BANANA COOKIES



Small bites of banana chocolate chip cookies!
  • Time|| 15min
  • Serves|| 1

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 Ripe Bananas
  • 2 Eggs
  • 260g of either Peanut Butter or Lotus Speculoos 'Butter'
  • Drizzle of Honey
  • Half a tablespoon of Baking Powder
  • A tablespoon of vanilla
  • Optional: Chocolate Chip Cookies

STEPS

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C / 400°F
  2. Mix everything except the chocolate chips together, once done, mix those in together as well.
  3. Put small amount into muffin tin, and if the oven is preheated, put in for eight minutes.
  4. Take out, let cool, and enjoy!
