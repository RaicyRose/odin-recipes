JACKET BAKED POTATOES



Jacket baked potatoes is an easy and hearty meal, ideal to keep warm during the cold winter nights.


INGREDIENTS

  • One Large Potato [ ~1/4KG ]
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Optional: Butter
  • Optional: Garlic Butter

STEPS

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C / 400°F
  2. Lightly coat the potato in olive oil, making sure it's spread evenly. Add a sprinkle of salt on the surface of the potato.
  3. Poke holes throughout the potato, to avoid any potential accidents when baking in the oven.
  4. Once the oven has finished preheating, put the potato in the oven for about one hour, or until the skin looks crispy.
    You can check if thhe potato by poking a hole in it to the center, if it goes in smoothly, it's done.
  5. Optional: When cutting the potato in half, don't cut all the way through. Open the potato up and spread your choice of butter, and close the potato. After a few minutes, open it again and enjoy!
